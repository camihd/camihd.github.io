    About

    Omar Camilo Hortua Diaz is a motivated and dynamic person. As a young engineer he looks forward to experiencing new projects and jobs where he can continue to grow in his professional life. He is also reliable and organized. He wants to continue developing more engineering skills along with his passion for design; combining a strong analytical mind, attention to detail and desire to learn. He is good at working as part of a team; though, he also likes to work on his own initiative, committing and giving all his effort to succeed in everything he performs.

    In November 2019 he defended his master's thesis ‘Design of structural profiles with interlaminar reinforcements in composites’ in Mechanical Engineering at the Universidad de Guanajuato. During his course development, he also worked together with four classmates and a physiotherapist in a patent application in Mexico ‘Rehabilitation device for walking with hip swing and interaction of the person's weight’.

    His research interests include polymer, reinforcements, additive, resource recovery and polymeric composites materials in general. He always look to improve and get a more profound knowledge realted to his research in materials.
